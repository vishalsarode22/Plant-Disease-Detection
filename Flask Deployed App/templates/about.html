🌿 About LeafMitra

AI Plant Disease Detection — Final Year Engineering Project

About the Project

LeafMitra is an AI-powered plant disease detection system developed as a Final Year Engineering Project by students of the Artificial Intelligence & Data Science department.

The system uses a custom Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on the PlantVillage dataset to identify diseases across multiple plant types and 39 disease categories. It integrates Google Gemini AI for detailed treatment insights and generates downloadable PDF reports.
